========================================================
1) ANDHRA PRADESH
========================================================
[Overview & Vibe]
Spiritual temples, long beaches and green hills. Mix of pilgrimage and coastal relaxation.

[Best Time]
Oct–Feb best; Apr–Jun very hot.

[Key Areas to Stay]
Tirupati, Visakhapatnam, Araku, Vijayawada.

[Top Attractions]
Tirupati Temple – Major Hindu shrine.
RK Beach Vizag – Popular city beach.
Araku Valley – Hill scenery and coffee plantations.

[Food]
Andhra thali, seafood in Vizag, spicy curries.

[Transport]
Airports: Vizag, Tirupati. Trains, buses, taxis.

[Culture]
Strict temple rules, vegetarian food near temples.

[Safety]
Crowds during festivals; safeguard valuables.

[Itinerary]
3 Days: Tirupati – Vizag – Araku.
